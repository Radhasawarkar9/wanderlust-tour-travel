Our Story

Ten Years ofExtraordinary

Who We Are

Born from aPassion for India

Wanderlust was founded in 2015 by Khushi Sawarkar — a traveller, storyteller, and deeply committed custodian of India's extraordinary cultural heritage. What began as a personal journey of discovery across Rajasthan's golden forts became a calling: to share India's soul with the world.

We are not just a travel agency. We are experience architects. Every itinerary is hand-crafted, every partner personally vetted, and every moment designed to create lifelong memories. From the snow-crowned passes of Ladakh to the tranquil backwaters of Kerala, we have spent a decade learning India's secrets — so you can experience them firsthand.

Today, our team of 18 passionate travel specialists has guided over 12,000 travellers across 85+ destinations, earning recognition from India's Ministry of Tourism and leading travel publications. We are proudly headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra — the geographic heart of India.

Our Mission
To Elevate Every Journey
Our mission is to craft journeys that transcend the ordinary — blending India's timeless heritage with modern luxury, responsible tourism, and deeply personalised service. We believe travel should be transformative, not transactional. Every trip we design carries our promise: that the experience will exceed your highest expectation.

By 2030, we aim to be India's most trusted boutique travel house, setting the benchmark for sustainable luxury tourism across the subcontinent.

Milestones

Our Journey ofAchievements

A decade of passion, persistence, and purpose — these are the moments that defined Wanderlust's journey from a one-person startup to India's most celebrated boutique travel house.

2015
Founded in Nagpur
Khushi Sawarkar launches Wanderlust with a vision to offer curated Indian journeys to discerning travellers. First 50 clients served in year one.
2017
Ministry of Tourism Recognition
Awarded the Responsible Tourism Certificate by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India — our first national recognition.
2019
1,000 Happy Travellers
Crossed the milestone of 1,000 satisfied clients. Expanded team to 8 specialists. Launched our signature 'Royal Rajasthan' package to critical acclaim.
2020
TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice
Earned TripAdvisor's coveted Travellers' Choice Award — an honour we have maintained every year since, reflecting our 98% satisfaction rate.
2022
Best Luxury Agency — India Travel Awards
Recognised as India's Best Luxury Travel Agency at the India Travel Awards ceremony in New Delhi, competing against 200+ agencies.
2024
12,000+ Journeys Crafted
Expanded to 85 destinations, launched international packages to Bhutan, Nepal & Sri Lanka, and won Best Luxury Agency for the second consecutive year.
